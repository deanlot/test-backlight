### Inferring busy state
<p>
In the below usage, the busy indicator will be displayed while the promise is being executed, once completed, te button will display the success icon briefly to the user.
</p>
<Playground
  scope={{ FeedbackButton }}
  code={`
  <FeedbackButton
    variant="primary"
    inferBusy
    onClick={() => new Promise((resolve) => setTimeout(resolve, 3000))}
  >
    Click Me
  </FeedbackButton>
  `}
/>

### Failure state
<p>
  Just like the promise-based version above, "onClick" can receive a synchronous callback that will be turned into a promise
  inside of the FeedbackButton. I think
</p>
<Playground
  scope={{ FeedbackButton }}
  code={`
  <FeedbackButton
    variant="primary"
    inferBusy
    onClick={() => new Promise((resolve, reject) => setTimeout(reject, 2000))}

    Click Me
  </FeedbackButton>
  `}
/>
